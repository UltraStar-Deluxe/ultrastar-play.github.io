UltraStar Play

Free and open source singing game for desktop and mobile.

The successor of UltraStar Deluxe, inspired by SingStar™. The game plays a song, displays lyrics, notes, and optionally a background video or image. Up to six players sing along and try to hit notes to earn points.

Companion App

Use your smartphone as mic or browse the song list with the UltraStar Play Companion App!

Solos, Duets, Groups

Sing a duet with a friend or make a party and sing with up to six players at the same time.

Playlists and Favorites

Handle large song collections by marking songs as favorites or adding playlists.

Thousands of Songs

There are many community created songs in the UltraStar format on the internet. By default, the game is packed with some Creative Commons licensed demo songs.

Song Editor

The integrated song editor allows to create own sing-along versions of any song.

Player Profiles

You can create a profile for every player and adjust the difficulty individually.

Mic Profiles

Configure each device individually and quickly switch mics between players.

Made with Unity

Built on a professional game engine, modern tools, and supporting a wealth of platforms.

Open source

UltraStar Play belongs to everyone. Get involved, add features, ideas, styles, or translations on GitHub.

More to Come

Planned features include: party modes, medleys, online multiplayer, downloadable themes, and improved UI.
